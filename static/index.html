Escrow Type Help
  • Timed Escrow Create - create an escrow that releases funds automatically after a specific future date.
  • Conditional Escrow Create - create an escrow that only releases funds when a required condition is fulfilled.
  • Oracle Price Threshold - create a price-based escrow using a token pair and target price to derive the release condition.
  • Escrow Finish - finish or release an existing escrow by providing the owner, sequence, and fulfillment details.
  • Token Payment - send issued tokens or amounts to a destination account.
  • Trustline - establish a trust line for an issued token from a specific issuer.
  • Issue Token - issue a token payment from the issuer account to a recipient.

Boundless Vault Setup Guide

Boundless Vault on XRPL

Welcome! Boundless Vault enables you to secure, manage, and execute complex smart contract transactions completely locally. Your private keys never touch any server.

All cryptographic preimages and seeds are generated locally.

Step 1: Connect with Xaman

Click **Connect Wallet** in the left sidebar to generate a secure login QR code. Review and sign the payload in your Xaman mobile application to grant read-only public access to your ledger state.

Signing in updates your live XRP balance and tracks active escrows automatically.

Step 2: Choose Your Template

Open **Build Escrow** to design lockups. Select **Timed Escrows** to release funds after a certain date, or **Conditional Escrows** to release funds when a secret fulfillment key is provided.

Use our integrated crypt-generator to precalculate keys.

Step 3: Monitor & Claim

Your personal dashboard shows active time-locks and expiry countdowns. Switch between **Grid View** and **Spreadsheet View** to scan details, and trigger one-click **Claim** or **Cancel** transactions directly!

You can re-run this setup tour anytime from the bottom sidebar menu.
